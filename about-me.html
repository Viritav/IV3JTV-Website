About Me

Hi! My name is Marco, I got my licence when I was 17 years old and I'm a passionate ham radio operator with the callsign IV3JTV. My adventure in the world of radio communications began when I was 16, and since then, I have never stopped exploring and learning. I am fascinated by the combination of electronics, computer science, and ham radio, which allows me to create innovative projects and connect with people around the world.

I have also been a member of the IQ3TS ham radio club in Trieste since this year, where I have had the opportunity to learn and share experiences with other enthusiasts.

In my free time, I enjoy experimenting with new electronic circuits, building antennas, and developing software that enhances the ham radio experience. I believe that sharing knowledge is fundamental, and that's why I created this website to help other enthusiasts discover and cultivate this wonderful hobby.

Besides radio, I am also a big fan of computer science and programming. I love solving complex problems and finding creative solutions. My goal is to continue growing and learning, sharing my discoveries and projects with the community.
